This scene contains a texture, available here:

Thanks to solarsystemscope for this texture.
Textures from this website are based on NASA elevation and imagery data. 
Colors and shades of the textures are tuned accordng to true-color photos made by Messenger, Viking and Cassini spacecrafts, and, of course, the Hubble Space Telescope.
